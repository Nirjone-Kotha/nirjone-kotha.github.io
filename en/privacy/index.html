Privacy Notice

Moner Kotha offers an optional password-only profile without collecting a real email address or phone number. Guests can continue with device-only storage.

Open appUse safely
Editable pre-launch draft: owner and legal review are still required.

Password-only Firebase profile

If you choose a password profile, the app creates a pseudonymous Firebase Authentication account and stores your app data under your private Firebase UID. You do not enter a real email address or phone number.

Guest mode and local storage

If you skip the password option, posts, comments, preferences and check-ins remain in the current browser. Clearing site data or changing devices means that guest profile cannot be restored.

Password responsibility

The password is the profile ID and access key. There is no recovery channel. Anyone who knows and uses the same password can access the same profile, so use a strong unique passphrase that is not reused elsewhere.

Emotional information is not an advertising signal

Mood, need, post text, comments, searches, saved content and safety interactions must not be used for ad targeting or remarketing.

Your controls

You can export app data, reset device data, and—when signed in—clear the synced app data associated with the password profile from Profile and settings.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to provide a phone number or real email?

No. The visitor profile asks only for a password and uses a pseudonymous Firebase identity.

Can a forgotten password be recovered?

No. There is no phone or email recovery channel, so the password must be remembered.

Are mood choices used for ads?

No. The advertising boundary explicitly forbids that use.
