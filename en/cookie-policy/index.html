Cookie and Local Storage Policy

Essential local storage keeps interface preferences and Guest activity on the device. An optional password profile also uses Firebase Authentication and Realtime Database for profile restoration.

Open appUse safely
Editable pre-launch draft: owner and legal review are still required.

Essential local storage

Language, theme, consent choices and current app state are stored locally. Guest posts and comments remain only in that browser.

Optional password profile storage

Choosing a password profile uses Firebase Authentication and Realtime Database to restore app data. No real email address or phone number is entered by the visitor.

Optional analytics

No analytics provider is configured. If one is added, it must wait for consent and must not receive emotional text, mood, search or safety details.

Optional advertising

Advertising is off. Future non-personalized ads require consent handling and will remain absent from sensitive parts of the platform.

Change your choice

The app includes a cookie settings control so optional categories can be revisited later.

Frequently asked questions

Are optional cookies active now?

No. Analytics and advertising consent are off by default.

Can I clear local data?

Yes, from Profile and settings.
