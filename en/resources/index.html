Emotional Wellbeing Resources

This launch page provides a transparent resource structure. It does not publish fabricated clinical advice or pretend unfinished articles are professionally reviewed.

Open appUse safely

Planned educational categories

Stress management, loneliness, anxiety education, relationship communication, workplace and study pressure, sleep habits, journaling, active listening, professional-help guidance and online safety.

Editorial requirements

Health-related articles must name the editorial team, show publish and review dates, provide references and clearly state that they are educational rather than medical advice.

Professional review must be real

No fake doctor, qualification or reviewer will be used. Pages requiring clinical review remain drafts until a qualified reviewer is engaged.

Start with platform features now

While editorial resources are developed, users can access the calm space, mood check-in, privacy guidance and community rules.

Frequently asked questions

Are these medical articles?

No. This is a resource directory and editorial plan until reviewed articles are published.

Can community posts be treated as sources?

No. User experiences are not medical evidence.
