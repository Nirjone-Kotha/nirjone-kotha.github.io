Sharing Stress and Overwhelm

Describe work, study, family or everyday pressure and find supportive posts and short calming exercises.

Open appUse safely

Separate the pressure into parts

Writing one concern at a time can make an overwhelming situation easier to describe.

Tell people whether you want ideas

Sometimes listening is more useful than advice. The post composer lets you state that preference.

Take a body pause

A short breathing or sensory reset can reduce immediate stimulation before returning to the problem.

Escalate beyond the app when necessary

Unsafe work, abuse, health symptoms or crisis situations need appropriate real-world help.

Frequently asked questions

Is this page medical advice?

No. It is educational community guidance and does not diagnose or treat a condition.

Can I share another person’s identity?

No. Remove identifying and contact information before posting.
