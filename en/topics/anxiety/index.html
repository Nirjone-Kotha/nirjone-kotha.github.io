Talking About Anxiety Without Judgement

A non-diagnostic community page for expressing anxious feelings, finding relatable posts and using short grounding tools.

Open appUse safely

Name the experience in your own words

You do not need a perfect label. Describe what is happening, what feels difficult, and what kind of response would help.

Choose support rather than certainty

Peers can listen and share gentle personal strategies, but should not diagnose or promise an outcome.

Use a short reset

Slow breathing or grounding may help create a pause while you decide what support to seek next.

Seek qualified help when needed

Severe, persistent or disabling symptoms should be discussed with a qualified professional.

Frequently asked questions

Is this page medical advice?

No. It is educational community guidance and does not diagnose or treat a condition.

Can I share another person’s identity?

No. Remove identifying and contact information before posting.
