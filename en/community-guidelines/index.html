Community Guidelines

A supportive community is created through everyday choices: how we listen, what we do not ask for, and when we report something unsafe.

Open appUse safely

Listen before solving

Acknowledge feelings first. Do not force advice, compare pain or tell someone they should simply be positive.

Protect identity and boundaries

Do not request phone numbers, addresses, photos, private meetings or social-media handles.

Keep dangerous details out

Never publish instructions, methods, challenges or content that glorifies self-harm or violence.

Report instead of escalating

Use the report tool for harassment, exploitation, dangerous details or privacy violations. Do not start a public argument.

Frequently asked questions

Can I give advice?

Only when it is invited, and it should remain gentle, practical and non-medical.

Can users exchange contact details?

No. The platform is designed to keep support inside moderated public features.
