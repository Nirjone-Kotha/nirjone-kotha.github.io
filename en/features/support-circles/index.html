Support Groups

Join topic-focused groups for shared situations such as work stress, study pressure, relationships or late-night loneliness.

Open appUse safely

Why groups help

A focused space makes it easier to find posts from people facing a similar situation.

Joining is lightweight

The local prototype stores group membership on the device; production membership will sync through an account.

Group boundaries

Groups are public support spaces, not private chatrooms or contact-exchange channels.

Moderation needs

Each group requires reporting, policy enforcement and moderator visibility before public scale.

Frequently asked questions

Is this feature available in the local prototype?

Yes, the core interaction is available locally; shared multi-user data requires the production backend.

Does it replace professional support?

No. It is a product feature for expression and peer support.
