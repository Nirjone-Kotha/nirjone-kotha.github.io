Anonymous Sharing

Share feelings through a chosen display name without publishing a photo, phone number or address.

Open appUse safely

Why anonymity can lower the first barrier

A chosen name can make it easier to begin writing while still allowing community continuity.

How to use it

Choose a display name, select a mood and support preference, write in your own language and review the privacy reminder before publishing.

What anonymity does not mean

It is not permission to harass, manipulate or evade moderation. Production accounts remain accountable to safety systems.

Privacy boundary

Personal contact details are blocked in the composer and should never be requested in replies.

Frequently asked questions

Is this feature available in the local prototype?

Yes, the core interaction is available locally; shared multi-user data requires the production backend.

Does it replace professional support?

No. It is a product feature for expression and peer support.
