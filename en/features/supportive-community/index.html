Supportive Community

Respond with listening, validation and gentle support instead of judgement, pressure or popularity competition.

Open appUse safely

Support reactions with meaning

Reactions communicate “I hear you” or “you matter” rather than ranking pain.

Guided comments

Reply prompts help people avoid dismissive or overly directive language.

No follower-status pressure

The experience is designed around useful connection, not influencer metrics.

Reporting protects the space

People can report harassment, unsafe details or requests for private contact.

Frequently asked questions

Is this feature available in the local prototype?

Yes, the core interaction is available locally; shared multi-user data requires the production backend.

Does it replace professional support?

No. It is a product feature for expression and peer support.
