Calm Space

Use short breathing, grounding, sensory reset, body awareness and reflection tools for a quieter moment.

Open appUse safely

Choose a low-pressure exercise

Start with breathing, a 60-second reset, grounding or a self-kindness prompt.

Designed for short pauses

Tools use clear steps, optional sound and reduced-motion support.

Keep expectations realistic

A brief exercise may help create space, but it cannot solve every cause of distress.

Know when to seek more help

Persistent or severe symptoms, injury or immediate danger require qualified or emergency support.

Frequently asked questions

Is this feature available in the local prototype?

Yes, the core interaction is available locally; shared multi-user data requires the production backend.

Does it replace professional support?

No. It is a product feature for expression and peer support.
