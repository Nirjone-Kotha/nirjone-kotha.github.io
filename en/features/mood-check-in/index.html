Mood Check-in

Choose the feeling closest to you and see relevant community posts first without turning mood into an advertising profile.

Open appUse safely

A quick way to notice your state

The check-in asks for one simple selection rather than a long assessment.

How feed ordering works

Matching mood posts are placed first, followed by other posts in their existing order.

Not a diagnosis

A mood label is a navigation and reflection aid, not a clinical assessment.

Privacy boundary

Mood and check-in history are excluded from advertising and sensitive analytics.

Frequently asked questions

Is this feature available in the local prototype?

Yes, the core interaction is available locally; shared multi-user data requires the production backend.

Does it replace professional support?

No. It is a product feature for expression and peer support.
