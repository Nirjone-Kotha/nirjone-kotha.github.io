Contact Moner Kotha

This page is for platform and customer support. It is not an emergency hotline or a crisis response channel.

Open appUse safely

What you can contact us about

Technical problems, account or privacy questions, reports about community behaviour, partnership enquiries and accessibility feedback.

Do not send urgent crisis messages here

If someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services and a trusted person nearby instead of waiting for a platform reply.

Contact details will activate from one configuration

Facebook, email and WhatsApp remain safely disabled until the owner adds verified values to the central site configuration.

Frequently asked questions

Why does a contact option say Coming soon?

The owner has not added a verified contact value yet. Empty or broken links are never shown.

Is this a counselling hotline?

No. These contacts are only for platform support.
