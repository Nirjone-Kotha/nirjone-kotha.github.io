Safety Information

Moner Kotha can help someone feel heard, but it cannot provide emergency response or replace qualified professional care.

Open appUse safely

If there is immediate danger

Contact the emergency service for the user’s location, move near a trusted person and seek urgent medical care for any injury or poisoning.

How the app responds to high-risk language

The prototype shows a safety-first screen and removes advertising from high-risk views. Production requires trained moderation and verified local resources.

What peers should do

Stay calm, listen, avoid method discussion, encourage nearby human help and report concerning content.

Protecting supporters too

People offering support should take breaks, avoid taking sole responsibility for another person and use moderator escalation when available.

Frequently asked questions

Does the app call emergency services automatically?

No. Users must contact emergency or trusted-person support directly.

Will high-risk posts show ads?

The architecture suppresses ads in high-risk and safety contexts.
