Moner Kotha

Moner Kotha is a calm social space for people who need somewhere to speak honestly, feel heard, or simply pause for a moment.

Open appUse safely

A private-feeling space without public identity pressure

Use a chosen display name instead of exposing a photo, phone number or address. The current test version keeps activity on the device; the production version will use a protected account and moderation system.

Share, listen, or ask for support

Write what is on your mind, choose whether you want replies, and respond with empathy. The platform is designed to reduce pressure rather than reward popularity.

Tools for a calmer moment

Use slow breathing, grounding, sensory reset and short reflection exercises. These tools support a pause; they do not replace medical or emergency care.

Designed for Bangla and English users

The interface can be changed between Bangla and English while posts and comments remain exactly in the language their authors used.

Frequently asked questions

Is Moner Kotha a therapy service?

No. It is a peer-support and self-expression platform, not a diagnosis, therapy, or emergency service.

Can I use a nickname?

Yes. A chosen display name is encouraged, and personal contact details should not be posted.

Can I install it as an app?

Yes. The same website can be installed as a PWA on supported phones and computers.
