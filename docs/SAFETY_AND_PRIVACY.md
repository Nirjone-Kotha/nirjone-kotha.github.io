# Safety and privacy notes

- Public identity is a temporary alias.
- Current composer blocks obvious phone numbers, email, URLs and social contact requests.
- Current urgent-language detector redirects to a safety screen; it is only a front-end aid and must not be treated as clinical assessment.
- No images or private messaging are enabled in the prototype.
- Final version needs human moderation, audit trails, rate limits, block/mute tools, region-specific verified help resources and a lawful data-retention policy.
- Emotional data must never be used for targeted advertising.
